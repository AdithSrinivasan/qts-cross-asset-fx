## Great Britain / United Kingdom: DEXUSUK
- File: `DEXUSUK.csv`
- Title: U.S. Dollar to British Pound Spot Exchange Rate
- Description: Daily exchange rate of U.S. Dollar (USD) to British Pound (GBP) from 1971-01-04 to 2026-02-20
- Units: U.S. Dollars to One U.K. Pound Sterling
- Source: Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)
- Link: [FRED DEXUSUK](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/DEXUSUK)
